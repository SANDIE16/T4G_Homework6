    African Plantain

    Plantain,major group of banana varieties (genus Musa) that are staple foods in many tropical areas. The edible fruits of plantain bananas has more starch than the common dessert banana and is not eaten raw. Because plantains have the most starch before they ripen,they are usually green,either boiled or fried,in savory dishes. The ripe fruits often sweet and are often cooked with coconut juive or sugar as a flavoring. Plantains may also be dried for later use in cooking or ground for use as a meal,which can be furthur refined to a flour.
